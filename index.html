About Me

Turning Imagination into Design

I’m a 24-year-old creative with over 4.5 years of experience as an illustrator, graphic designer, and creative director. I love crafting designs that leave a lasting impact—bringing ideas to life through color, playfulness, and strong storytelling. My style is vibrant and fun, but I’ve also learned to adapt and soften it to align with each brand’s unique voice. Throughout my career, I’ve worked on a diverse range of projects including logo design, brand identity, social media content, and both digital and hand-drawn illustrations. I believe great design goes beyond visuals—it’s about creating something memorable that tells a story.
