Son of Adam

Menu Card

Project Date October , 2025
Role Desiging, Branding
Client Son of Adam

Son of Adam is a premium men’s grooming brand built around the ideals of modern masculinity—confidence, refinement, and authenticity. The visual identity centers on a sophisticated monogram that blends the letters S and A into a fluid, sculptural mark. Its gold finish conveys luxury and heritage, while the minimalist composition keeps the brand feeling contemporary and clean. The overall aesthetic balances royal elegance with modern simplicity, reflecting the brand’s promise of high-quality, organic grooming essentials. Soft gradients and ample negative space were used throughout the system to communicate calmness, purity, and a premium self-care experience. Typography is understated and refined, reinforcing the brand’s quiet confidence. This branding system positions Son of Adam as a trusted, upscale choice for men who value both style and substance—an effortless blend of classic masculinity and modern grooming culture.
